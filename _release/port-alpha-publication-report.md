## Scope

- The 1.18.2–1.20.1 Forge ports and the 1.16.5 Forge port include new optional compatibility hooks selected from the integration ecosystem available for each Minecraft version.
- The Fabric 1.20.1 port includes the integrations available for that loader, including Create Fabric as its required dependency.
- The 1.12.2 legacy alpha remains core/API-only. Matching legacy magnet and vacuum mods were researched and documented, but no unsafe untested coremod or reflection hooks are claimed.
- Every uploaded jar completed its final build before CurseForge returned the file ID above.
- Newly added compatibility hooks are experimental and should be treated as alpha support until they receive broader in-game testing.

CurseForge may show accepted files as processing or under review before they become publicly downloadable.
